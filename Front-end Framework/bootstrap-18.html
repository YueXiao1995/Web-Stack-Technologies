1. Simple Collapsible

The .collapse class indicates a collapsible element (a <div> in our example); this is the content that will be shown or hidden with a click of a button. To control (show/hide) the collapsible content, add the data-toggle="collapse" attribute to an <a> or a <button> element. Then add the data-target="#id" attribute to connect the button with the collapsible content

Click on the button to toggle between showing and hiding content.
